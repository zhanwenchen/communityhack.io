FAQ

  • What is CommunityHack?

    CommunityHack is a student-run hackathon at Vassar College, where students will have two days to learn how to code and/or work with your friends to make your idea come to life. CommunityHack's core mission is to provide a space where people of all coding levels can come to learn how to code, and/or build innovative technology which can help communities affected by marginalization, oppression, corruption, etc

  • When is CommunityHack?

    CommunityHack will start on Friday, March 31 and run until Saturday, April 1. If you need to arrive late or leave early, please let us know ahead of time.

  • Who can attend?

    Anyone in high school or college with an interest in coding! High Schoolers under 18 years old must bring a permission slip in order to attend. Make sure to register beforehand!

  • Is there any cost?

    Nope! CommunityHack is completely free!

  • Is there a deadline?

    No deadline, just make sure to register before the event! Or if you decide to drop in, register when you get there.

  • Do you have a code of conduct?

    Yes, good question! We have adopted the MLH code of conduct , which all attendees will be required to adhere to.

  • Isn't hacking illegal?

    In the context of a hackathon, "hacking" is to build a real application over the length of the event. It's important to not confuse this with illegal hacking, which is gaining unauthorized access to a computer.

  • Do I need a team?

    If you already have a team of 2-4 people you want to work with, that's awesome! If not don't worry because we'll have an opportunity for people to join a team if they need one.

  • What if I don't know how to code?

    Come to CommunityHack! Coding is fun, creative, and an important skill, and CommunityHack is the perfect way to learn. We'll have workshops and mentors to help guide you.

  • What should I bring?

    You could bring a laptop if you want but we'll have computers available with a lot of development software already downloaded and installed on it! This isn't an overnight hackathon, so no need for sleeping gear!

  • Will there be food?

    Yes. Lots and lots of food.

  • What if I have dietary concerns?

    We'll be providing meals for most everyone at CommunityHack, but if you have special concerns please let us know .

If your question isn't answered here, just ask us: hackathon@cs.vassar.edu .
